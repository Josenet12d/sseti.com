About Us · SSETI

South Sudan Educational Technology Initiative: Our story, mission, and commitment to national prosperity through digital literacy.

Preamble

We, the founders and members of the South Sudan Educational Technology Initiative (SSETI), recognizing the critical need for educational transformation in South Sudan through technological innovation, and acknowledging the power of digital literacy to empower future generations, hereby establish this constitution. We are committed to bridging the digital divide, fostering innovation, and creating sustainable educational solutions that will propel South Sudan toward a knowledge-based economy and a prosperous future for all its citizens.

Abstract

This document constitutes the official Constitution of the South Sudan Educational Technology Initiative (SSETI), a non-profit, non-governmental organization established to drive educational transformation in the Republic of South Sudan through technological innovation. Adopted to provide a formal governance and operational framework, the constitution outlines SSETI's core identity, strategic direction, and structural blueprint.

SSETI operates under the motto "Digital Literacy for National Prosperity" and is founded on the vision of a South Sudan where every learner and educator has equitable access to quality education and essential digital tools.

Background

South Sudan, the world's youngest nation, stands at a pivotal crossroads, rich with promise yet challenged by a profound educational and digital divide. With over 70% of its population under 30, this immense demographic dividend remains untapped, locked away by overcrowded classrooms, scarce resources, and a critical lack of connectivity, where mobile broadband penetration languishes below 25% (World Bank, 2023).

SSETI was founded as a direct, strategic response to this national imperative, built on the conviction that the purposeful integration of educational technology is the essential accelerant to transform latent potential into a dynamic engine for innovation, peace, and a knowledge-based future.

Primary Objectives

Digital Infrastructure

Deploy and maintain digital learning infrastructure in underserved schools and community centers.

Educator Training

Build capacity of educators through certified training in digital pedagogy and EdTech integration.

Open Educational Resources

Develop, curate, and promote OER relevant to the South Sudanese curriculum and context.

Youth Innovation Hubs

Establish coding clubs, maker-spaces for youth to develop digital creativity and entrepreneurial skills.

Policy Advocacy

Advocate for and inform national policies supporting technology integration in education.

Research

Conduct and disseminate research on the effectiveness of educational technology interventions in South Sudan.

Strategic Goals (6-Year Horizon)

  • Train 5,000 teachers in digital literacy and EdTech integration.
  • Establish 50 fully functional digital learning labs across all 10 states.
  • Develop a national online repository of localized digital learning materials.
  • Facilitate the launch of 100 youth-led tech innovation projects.
  • Achieve financial sustainability through a diversified funding model.

Our Approach: Appropriate & Sustainable Innovation

SSETI's mission is therefore defined by a philosophy of appropriate and sustainable innovation, designing context-specific solutions for South Sudan's unique realities. Informed by landscape analyses that highlight the success of low-bandwidth, resilient EdTech models in fragile contexts, we bypass infrastructural constraints by pioneering offline digital libraries, solar-powered learning labs, and mobile-first platforms. This ensures our interventions are practical, scalable, and meet the acute needs of underserved communities.

This grounded approach is coupled with an unwavering commitment to local ownership, investing deeply in certifying South Sudanese educators and curating localized digital content aligned with the national curriculum (MoGEI), ensuring solutions are developed by and for South Sudanese.

"SSETI is not merely delivering technology—it is systematically building the foundational digital literacy required for South Sudan to achieve enduring stability and self-reliant prosperity."
